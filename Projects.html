Mol-Hirfa

The "Mol-Hirfa" app aims to connect artisans with customers, allowing customers to rate the artisans available nearby. The app provides an easy way for customers to find the right artisans for their needs and to rate their services based on their experiences. On the other hand, it helps artisans reach a wider customer base and increase their opportunities for getting new work.

Insulin Dose

The "Insulin Dose" app offers a comprehensive and effective solution for managing diabetes. It combines advanced tracking features with reminders for medication and meal times. Users can instantly log their blood sugar levels and analyze the data to understand trends, receiving precise reminders that contribute to better diabetes management and improved health for users.
